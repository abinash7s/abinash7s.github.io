About

Background

I am a PhD student in Theoretical Physics at the University of the Witwatersrand, Johannesburg. My research sits at the confluence of three rapidly evolving fields — asking how the tools of machine learning can illuminate problems in quantum theory, and conversely, how quantum principles might reshape computation and learning.

I maintain a living research map — an interactive Venn diagram cataloguing papers across these intersecting domains, organised by the regions of overlap they inhabit.
