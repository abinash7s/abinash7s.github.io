Abinash Swain

PhD student in theoretical physics at the University of Witwatersrand. My research focuses on the intersection of Theoretical Physics, Machine Learning, and Quantum Information.

Research Interests

  • Machine Learning for Quantum Systems
  • Neural Quantum States
  • Matrix Models and Gauge Theories
  • Quantum Information and Complexity

Current Focus

My current work explores neural-network representations of quantum many-body systems and matrix models, combining ideas from variational Monte Carlo, generative models, and gauge symmetries.
